## Features

### The Vibe Engine
Most editors let you change fonts. SummerPad changes your **reality**.
* **The Romantic:** *Playfair Display* on warm beige. For poetry and love letters.
* **The Hacker:** *JetBrains Mono* on deep black. For code snippets and manifestos.
* **The Cyberpunk:** *Righteous* with **Neon Glow**. For 3 AM thoughts.
* **The Screenwriter:** *Courier Prime* on draft paper. For your next movie script.
* **The Ghost:** *Shadows Into Light*. For messy, anxious scribbling
* **Local-First:** Your data lives in `localStorage`.
* **No Cloud:** We don't see what you write.
* **Offline Ready:** Works without internet.
